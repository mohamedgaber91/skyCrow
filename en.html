    Project Idea

    The SkyCrow project is innovative in its concept as it directly relies on travelers to transport small and medium-sized shipments using their available weight. This idea significantly contributes to energy savings and recycling of unused resources for each traveler.

    App Workflow

    1.

    The person who wants to ship enters all shipment information.

    2.

    The person selects a traveler based on available app information (trip, traveler rating, etc.).

    3.

    The traveler accepts or declines the request based on shipment type and other criteria.

    4.

    The person pays the shipping fee, which SkyCrow holds until shipment delivery is confirmed. SkyCrow then pays the traveler after deducting the commission.

    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Responsibilities of travelers for shipments they carry

      • Travelers must keep shipments in the same condition as received.
      • Travelers must deliver shipments to the agreed-upon location.
      • In case of shipment loss, the traveler is directly responsible for compensating the owner.

    • What happens if the traveler pays customs fees for the shipment

      • For commercial shipments, the owner pays customs.
      • For personal shipments wrongly classified, the traveler pays customs.
      • If the mistake is the traveler’s, SkyCrow will pay customs for the shipment owner.
